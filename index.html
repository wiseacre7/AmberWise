ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Amber Wise. I am a Graphic Designer with 3 years experience working in the printing and mailing industry. I am MDP Certified (Mail Design Professional) with the USPS and have worked on countless mailpieces, flyers, and personal art projects to keep my design skills fresh. I have also worked in Software Development, Data Processing, and HelpDesk Analysis. In 2016, I graduated from Eastern Washington University where I earned my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with a Minor in 3D Animation. This website exists to showcase some of my creative work in a portfolio, so feel free to look around!
